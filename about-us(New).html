About Us

Started in November 2014 in Malaysia, E-Rent is a digital community platform for students & foreigners to find and book for accommodation.


Our goal is to allow students and foreigners that have common interests in renting rooms of the same unit to do so while leaving the middleman out of the equation. Users can also interact with potential housemates on the website before renting the rooms. This allows customers to gain savings while getting to know their future roomates before making their decisions.


We have thousands of verified rooms and apartments available, provided by private hostel owners and educational institutions, and we bring them all together in a safe and convenient environment.
